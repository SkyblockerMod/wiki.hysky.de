## 🔹 Key Features:
- [🏰 **Dungeons**](features/dungeons)  – Solvers, waypoints, profit calculators.
- [🐉 **Kuudra**](features/kuudra) – Waypoints, warnings, and glow indicators.  
- [🟥 **Crimson Isles**](features/crimson-isles) – Dojo helper and fog radius customization.  
- [🛠️ **Item & Armor Customization**](features/item-armor) – Rename items, custom dyes, and animated trims.  
- [❤️ **Health & Status Bars**](features/status-bars) – Custom health, mana, defense, and XP bars.  
- [📊 **Profile Viewer**](features/profile-viewer) – Skills, slayer, catacombs, inventory, and collections.  
- [⛏️ **Mining (Dwarven Mines & Crystal Hollows)**](features/mining) – Solvers, waypoints, HUDs, and tools.  
- [🌀 **Rift Features**](features/rift) – Mirrorverse waypoints and special glow effects.  
- [🕷️ **Spider's Den**](features/spiders-den) – Hidden relic finder.  
- [⚔️ **Slayer Helper**](features/slayer) – Vampire, Enderman, and Blaze Slayer enhancements.  
- [🌱 **Garden Features**](features/garden) – Farming HUD, visitor helper, and speed presets.  
- [🌟 **Visual Enhancements**](features/visuals) – AOTE smoothing, compact damage, fancy UI, and more.  
- [🖥️ **User Interface**](features/ui) – Inventory search, quick navigation, tooltips, and HUD widgets.  
- [🌾 **Barn Features**](features/barn) – Treasure Hunter and Hungry Hiker solvers.  
- [💬 **Chat Features**](features/chat) – Custom rules, autocomplete, and message filtering.  
- [🔍 **Miscellaneous Solvers**](features/misc-solvers) – Fishing helper, Jerry timer, Mythological ritual assistance.  
- [🛸 **Miscellaneous Features**](features/misc) – Discord Rich Presence, wiki lookup, Skyblock XP messages.
