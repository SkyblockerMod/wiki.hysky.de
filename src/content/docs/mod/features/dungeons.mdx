## 🎉 Party & Score Tracking
- **Fancy Party Finder GUI** – Enhances party finder UI.
- **Minimap & Score Display** – Shows dungeon score and map.
- **Starred Mob Glow** – Highlights important mobs.

## 🔎 Secrets & Waypoints
- **Secrets Waypoint Highlight** – Customizable secret location indicators.
- **AOTV & Pearl Secret Waypoints** – Guides you to key locations.
- **Player Secrets Tracker** – Displays secrets discovered per player.

## 🛠️ Additional Enhancements
- **Chest Profit Calculator** – Helps decide whether to open chests.
- **Reparty Command (`/rp`)** – Automatically rejoins dungeons.
- **Wither & Blood Door Highlight** – Clearly marks essential doors.
- **Block Incorrect Terminal Clicks** – Prevents accidental mistakes.
